About Me

Hello and welcome to my digitial travel log documenting my study abroad adventure in Ireland. My name is Sophie, and I will be your tour guide. This journey will take you the bustling streets of Dublin, the historic town of Kilkenny, the vibrant city of Galway, and the breathtaking Cliffs of Moher. Join me as I explore the rich culture, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality of the Emerald Isle.
